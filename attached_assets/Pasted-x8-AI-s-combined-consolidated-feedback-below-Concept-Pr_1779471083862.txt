	3. One financial snapshot tool with shared inputs, live visual outputs (update instantly as sliders move), optional deep-dive modules, and a downloadable branded summary.

Value-Added Innovations - We can elevate this even further by adding these powerful tools:
	1. The FIRE Benchmark: (Financial Independence, Retire Early) calculates their "FIRE Number" (25x annual expenses) and shows how close they are to achieving it in real-time.
	2. The Debt Snowball vs. Avalanche Toggle: Lets users see the visual difference (in time and money saved) between paying off smallest balances first vs. highest interest rates first.
	3. Rent vs. Buy Simulator: Instantly calculates whether purchasing a property (using the Bond Calculator) yields a higher net asset position over 10 years compared to renting and investing the difference.

Worth considering: balance them with at least one or two positive-framing storytellers:
	1. The Compound Win — "Your R3 000/month becomes R X million" (celebratory, not warning)
	2. Your Freedom Number — "At your current savings rate, you reach financial independence at age X"
	3. The Snowball Story — debt payoff but framed as victories, not warnings
