## Summary

AI integration is practical, affordable, and high-value for Advisory Connect at its current scale. Three options are ready to build now. Two require more platform maturity first. All use the Anthropic API (Claude) — the same AI that powers this development session.

**Estimated API cost at 50 advisors, 500 leads/month: under R50/month total.**

## Option 1 — Lead Intelligence Summary ⭐ Recommended First Build

**What it does:**
When an advisor opens a lead in their registry, an AI-generated insight panel appears alongside the lead card. It reads the lead's data (income, age, services requested, temperature, grade, lifestyle flags) and produces:
- A 2–3 sentence plain-English summary of who this person is
- 2–3 personalised talking points for the advisor's first call
- A suggested approach (e.g. "Lead is retirement-focused with high income — lead with RA and living annuity options")

**Example output:**
> *"Sarah is a 42-year-old married professional in the R45k–R60k income bracket with two children and a vehicle. She's requesting investment and retirement planning advice — a strong candidate for a retirement annuity review and education fund discussion. Approach: open with the two-pot retirement system changes and ask about her current RA contributions."*

**Why build this first:**
- Highest immediate value to advisors — makes every lead actionable
- Lowest risk — it's a read-only suggestion, no automation
- Cheap: ~500 tokens per lead summary = ~$0.001 per call
- Can be gated to Professional tier and above as a differentiator

**Effort:** Medium — 1–2 days for Friday to build the UI + API call. Backend prompt engineering is straightforward.

**Where it lives:** Lead detail panel in the AdvisorPanel, triggered on expand. Optional — advisor can dismiss or ignore.

## Option 2 — Pre-Qualification Chat on Contact Card ⭐ High Impact

**What it does:**
A small chat widget on the advisor's public contact card. Before a visitor submits a callback or referral form, the bot asks 3–4 qualifying questions in plain conversational language:
- "What are you mainly looking for help with today?"
- "Roughly what income bracket are you in?"
- "Are you looking for personal or business advice?"

The answers pre-fill the form fields and improve the Grader 2.0 score accuracy. Visitors who don't want to chat can skip straight to the form.

**Why build this:**
- Improves lead quality before it even hits the registry
- Reduces friction for visitors who find forms intimidating
- Differentiates Advisory Connect from static profile pages

**Effort:** Medium-high — needs a chat UI component, a streaming API call, and form pre-fill integration. Probably 3–4 days for Friday.

**Where it lives:** Floating chat button on the public contact card. Toggle on/off in advisor panel settings (gated to Pro+).

**Cost:** ~800 tokens per conversation = ~$0.002 per chat. Negligible.

## Option 3 — Grader 2.0 Qualitative Enhancement

**What it does:**
The current Grader 2.0 is purely formula-based (Income 35 + Age 20 + Lifestyle 20 + Services 15 + Source 10 = 100). An AI layer reads the free-text fields (message, notes, referral context) and adds a qualitative modifier to the score — rewarding leads who express urgency, financial stress, or specific life events in their own words.

**Example:**
A lead who writes *"My father just passed and I need to sort out the estate urgently"* would score higher than the formula alone would suggest, because the context signals immediate, high-value need.

**Effort:** Low — 1 day. Add an AI call at lead creation time, pass the message field, receive a modifier (-5 to +10), apply it to the score. Transparent to the advisor.

**When to build:** After Option 1 is live and advisors trust the AI suggestions. Grading is sensitive — introduce it carefully.

## Option 4 — Book of Life Document Analysis (Future)

**What it does:**
When an advisor uploads a client's policy documents to the Book of Life, AI reads the PDF and extracts key information: policy type, insurer, premium, sum assured, beneficiaries, review date. Populates a structured client summary automatically.

**Why not yet:**
- Book of Life hasn't been built yet
- PDF parsing adds complexity (needs a document extraction layer)
- Highest cost of the options listed (~5,000 tokens per document = ~$0.025 per upload)
- High value, but build Book of Life first, then layer this in

**Revisit:** When Book of Life (W2.4) is complete.

## Option 5 — Smart Lead Routing (Future)

**What it does:**
For Practice tier accounts with multiple advisors, AI matches incoming leads to the most suitable advisor based on the lead's profile, location, and service needs vs each advisor's specialisation.

**Why not yet:**
- Requires multiple advisors on one account (Practice tier)
- Not enough data yet to train meaningful matching
- Revisit when you have 3+ advisors on a single practice account

## Technical Notes

- **API:** Anthropic Claude API (`claude-haiku-4-5` for speed/cost on summaries, `claude-sonnet-4-6` for chat)
- **Key needed:** `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` environment variable in Replit/production
- **Latency:** Haiku responses in ~0.5–1s, Sonnet in ~1–3s — both acceptable for UI
- **Cost control:** Add a per-advisor monthly AI call budget cap server-side to prevent runaway costs
- **Privacy:** Lead data sent to the API should be stripped of PII (name, email, phone) — the scoring inputs (income bracket, age, services) are not personally identifying
