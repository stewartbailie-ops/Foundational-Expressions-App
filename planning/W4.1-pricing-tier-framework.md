## Recommended Model: Monthly Subscription Per Advisor

Advisors pay Advisory Connect a monthly fee to access the platform. Simple, predictable, and standard in SA SaaS.

**Annual billing option:** 1 month free — offered as a toggle on sign-up.

---

## Tier Structure

### Starter — R199/month
*For the new or part-time advisor testing the platform*

**Profile & Card**
- 1 advisor profile (primary only)
- Public contact card with full customisation (theme, photo, bio, socials)
- QR code
- WhatsApp share + Save Contact
- Emergency contacts section
- Document upload link (FAIS, brochure) — standard on all tiers from registration

**Lead Capture**
- Callback request form (reCAPTCHA protected)
- Lead registry — up to 30 active leads
- Email notifications on new leads
- Basic lead grade display (Gold/Silver/Bronze/Development)

**Limits**
- 1 profile
- 30 lead cap (older leads auto-archive when limit is reached)
- Advisory Connect branding on card footer

---

### Professional — R349/month
*For the active, established advisor*

**Everything in Starter, plus:**

**Profile & Card**
- Sub-profiles (up to 2 secondary profiles — different audiences, services, or areas)
- Platform links (My Liberty, Stanlib, SigningHub — per-toggle)
- Complimentary Will form
- Referral form
- Document upload link (FAIS, brochure)
- Astute link

**Lead Management**
- Unlimited leads
- Grader 2.0 full scoring (income, age, lifestyle, services, source)
- Lead temperature (Hot / Warm / Cold)
- Temperature glow trim in registry
- CSV export
- Lead archiving (separate archive tab)
- Enhanced timestamps (received, first viewed, last viewed)
- Registry sort, search, date filter, unread filter

**Content & Engagement**
- Financial tools suite (Tax, CGT, Compound, Pension, Exchange, Vehicle, Latte Factor)
- Interactive calculators + showpieces
- Moneyweb news feed
- Daily financial fun facts (6/day, from 300-fact pool)
- Live forex widget (USD/EUR/GBP per ZAR)

**Analytics**
- Full advisor stats dashboard (leads by type, grade breakdown, weekly activity)
- Profile view count

---

### Practice — R699/month
*For a financial planning practice with multiple advisors*

**Everything in Professional, plus:**

**Multi-Advisor**
- Up to 5 advisor seats included
- Additional advisors: R99/seat/month
- Master admin CIV dashboard (view and manage all advisors' leads) ⚠️ *Discuss with Zeeman*
- Bulk lead management (grade, archive, export across all advisors)

**Advanced Features**
- Secondary news feed (choose second source alongside Moneyweb)
- Book of Life — client portal (when built): promote leads to clients, store policy documents
- Priority email support
- Early access to new features

**Branding**
- Advisory Connect footer branding removed from cards ⚠️ *Discuss with Zeeman*

## Upgrade Triggers (when to prompt an advisor to move up)

| Trigger | Suggest |
|---|---|
| Lead count approaches 30 | Upgrade to Professional |
| Advisor asks about sub-profiles | Upgrade to Professional |
| Advisor wants CSV export | Upgrade to Professional |
| Practice wants multiple advisors on one account | Upgrade to Practice |
| Practice wants white-label or API | Upgrade to Enterprise |

## What to Decide Before Publishing

1. **Is the current Zeeman / founding advisor on a special rate?** If yes, document it privately.
2. **Free trial?** Recommended: 14-day free trial on Professional, no credit card required. ⚠️ *Discuss with Zeeman*
3. **Will you grandfather existing advisors** on their current rate if you raise prices later? Recommended: yes, for 12 months.
4. **Payment processor:** Peach Payments or PayFast are the standard SA options for ZAR subscriptions. PayFast is simpler to set up. Both integrate well with most stacks.
